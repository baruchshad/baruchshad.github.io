When Work Is More Than Just a Job

The Zahn Innovation Center

Working at the Zahn Innovation Center really give me the skills to manage and plan projects. I was able to expand my knowledge of business from our Co-Executive Director Devin Voorsanger and learn key managment skills from our program manager Anna Hutcheson. I created and improved excel files for tracking invoices, and created forms to streamline prototyping services.

Yaalozu Wilderness Program

Yaalozu Wilderness Survival Program directed by Joseph Sirote is geared toward teenage dropouts with social and emotional difficulties. As a division head I inspired and helped the campers grow their self confidence and teaching the power of teamwork. I was responsible for the well being of each camper ensuring their safety and success in the program. After my first year of being division head I was able to get all the campers to enroll for the coming year.

Marilyn David IVDU Upper School

This program is designed to prepare young adults with disabilities to transition into adult life by maximizing their strengths and preparing them for the work world. As an Instructor for the school I designed and executed a curriculum to teach students basic C++ programming. I taught them how to breakdown large tough problems into smaller easier ones, and how to debug code as problems arise. I was able to inspire students to explore topics that were not taught in class and apply them to the programs they wrote. As a mission of Ivdu I also explained how they can take these same skills of programming and apply it to their everyday lives.
